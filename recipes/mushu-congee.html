This hearty porridge is way a great dish to start the day. The filling rice, protein-rich eggs and salty bacon will keep you fueled for hours. Plus, it's always nice to wake up to a smiley face!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups leftover recipes
  • 1 litre chicken stock
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 slice of bacon
  • Bug (optional but highly recommended)

Steps

  1. To make the Congee combine rice and chicken stock in a pot and simmer for about 15 minutes on low to medium heat, stirring occasionally.
  2. As the congee becomes your desired consistency, turn heat off and set aside.
  3. Next, fry your bacon slice and sunny side up eggs.
  4. Place eggs and bacon on top of the congee and serve immediately.
